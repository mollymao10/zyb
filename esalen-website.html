About Gestalt Singing

The Gestalt Singing workshop at Esalen Institute offers a transformative approach to singing that integrates body, mind, and spirit. Gestalt principles emphasize awareness, presence, and authenticity — all essential elements of truly expressive singing.

Through this unique methodology, participants discover how to move beyond technical limitations and connect with their authentic voice. Whether you're a professional singer or someone who has been told you "can't sing," this workshop provides a safe space to explore and expand your vocal expression.

Our approach focuses on the healing aspects of vocalization, helping participants work through emotional blocks, enhance self-expression, and develop a deeper connection with themselves and others through the power of voice.
